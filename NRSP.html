National Rabies Surveillance Platform (NRSP)

The National Rabies Surveillance Platform (NRSP), developed by AHRI, serves as a centralized database and reporting system to track the occurrence of rabies cases across the United States. By collecting data from state/local health departments and other sources, the platform provides a nationwide overview to help public health and veterinary professionals identify trends, detect outbreaks, and coordinate response efforts related to this deadly zoonotic disease. This information is crucial for guiding prevention strategies and ensuring timely interventions to protect both human and animal populations.
