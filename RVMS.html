Rabies Vaccine Management System (RVMS)

The Rabies Vaccine Management System (RVMS) is a digital platform that streamlines rabies vaccine management and distribution in Ethiopia. By tracking inventory, monitoring demand, and optimizing distribution, RVMS addresses challenges like stockouts and geographical barriers. This tool enhances public health by enabling timely and equitable access to rabies vaccines, reducing the disease's incidence and protecting communities nationwide.

Issues Solved by RVMS

Vaccine Access

Addresses frequent vaccine stockouts at health facilities, reducing delays in administering life-saving treatment.

Real-Time Data

Implements a robust system to track vaccine inventory and demand, improving efficient management and timely resupply.

Public Awareness

Increases knowledge about rabies prevention and the importance of seeking immediate treatment following exposure.

Geographical Barriers

Improves vaccine distribution across Ethiopia's vast and diverse landscape, reaching all at-risk populations.

What We Do

Vaccine Production and R&D

Develop and produce effective rabies vaccines, including Fermi-type rabies vaccines, tailored to meet the needs of the Ethiopian population.

Diagnostics Enhancement

Advance diagnostic technologies to improve the detection and management of rabies cases across the country.

Public Awareness Campaigns

Educate the public on rabies prevention, the importance of vaccination, and the need for immediate treatment following exposure.

Laboratory Animal Research

Breed laboratory animals for vaccine testing and research, ensuring the effectiveness and safety of rabies vaccines.

Future Goals

National Scale-Up

Scale up the production and distribution of rabies vaccines nationwide, ensuring access in all regions.

Enhanced Surveillance

Implement advanced tracking systems for vaccine inventory and demand to prevent stockouts and improve management.

Continued Education

Expand outreach efforts to increase awareness of rabies prevention and the importance of timely PEP.

Research and Innovation

Invest in R&D to produce more effective vaccines and diagnostics, reducing rabies incidence and mortality.

Our Impact

Saving Lives

Reduce the current mortality rate of 2,700 deaths per year through improved vaccine management and increased public awareness.

Protecting Communities

Strengthen health facilities' capacity to respond effectively to rabies exposure cases, ensuring timely post-exposure prophylaxis (PEP).

Building a Sustainable System

Provide valuable insights into rabies trends through data collection and analysis, informing future interventions.

Empowering Communities

Foster a culture of proactive prevention through public awareness campaigns and community engagement.
