Integrated National Rabies Vaccine Management System (INRVMS)

The Integrated National Rabies Vaccine Management System (INRVMS) is an AHRI project that streamlines rabies vaccine management and distribution. The system enhances accessibility, reliability, and effectiveness of vaccination programs, supporting countries' efforts to eliminate rabies. INRVMS focuses on real-time inventory tracking, efficient logistics, data integration, and capacity building for healthcare workers and volunteers. By implementing INRVMS, countries can optimize their rabies control initiatives and work towards eliminating this public health threat.

Projects

Human Vaccine Management System

A Human Vaccine Management System is a digital platform that streamlines the administration, tracking, and optimization of vaccine distribution and utilization. It manages the entire vaccine lifecycle, from inventory to appointments, to enhance vaccination campaigns and protect public health.

Animal Vaccination Management System

An Animal Vaccination Management System is a digital platform that helps veterinary professionals efficiently manage the vaccination of animals. It tracks inventory, schedules appointments, maintains records, and generates reports, improving the reliability and effectiveness of animal vaccination programs.
