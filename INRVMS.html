Integrated National Rabies Vaccine Management System (INRVMS)

The Integrated National Rabies Vaccine Management System (INRVMS) is a project developed by AHRI to streamline the management and distribution of rabies vaccines. The system aims to enhance the accessibility, reliability, and effectiveness of rabies vaccination programs, supporting countries in their efforts to eliminate this deadly viral disease. The INRVMS focuses on key components such as real-time inventory tracking, efficient logistics for vaccine distribution, data integration to support evidence-based decision-making, and capacity building for healthcare professionals and community volunteers. By implementing the INRVMS, countries can optimize the impact of their rabies control initiatives and work towards the goal of eliminating this public health threat.

Human Vaccine Management System

A Human Vaccine Management System is a digital platform that streamlines the administration, tracking, and optimization of vaccine distribution and utilization. It manages the entire vaccine lifecycle, from inventory to appointments, to enhance vaccination campaigns and protect public health.

Animal Vaccination Management System

An Animal Vaccination Management System is a digital platform that helps veterinary professionals efficiently manage the vaccination of animals. It tracks inventory, schedules appointments, maintains records, and generates reports, improving the reliability and effectiveness of animal vaccination programs.
